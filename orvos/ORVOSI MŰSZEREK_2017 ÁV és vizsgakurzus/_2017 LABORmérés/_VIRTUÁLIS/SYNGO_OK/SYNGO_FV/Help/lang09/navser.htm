Navigating by Series

Next Series

·Click on Next Series  
 

Stack Mode: The next series is displayed in the upper left corner of the screen. The selection frame moves to the next stack.  

Doing this on the last field in the bottom right corner of the screen moves all of the images displayed in the main window to the background and displays the next series in the upper left corner of the screen.  

Stripe Mode: The currently displayed images are cleared and all of the images in the next series displayed. The first image in the next series is displayed in the upper left corner of the screen.             
If the selection frame is on the last image in the series, the message First series is displayed in the status bar of the main window.  
 

Previous Series

·Click on Previous Series  
 
   
Stack Mode: The previous series is displayed. The selection frame moves to the previous stack.  

Doing this on the last field in the upper left corner of the screen moves all of the images displayed in the main window to the background and displays the images from the previous series in the bottom right corner of the screen.  

Stripe Mode: The currently displayed images are cleared and all of the images in the previous series displayed. The first image in the next series is displayed in the upper left corner of the screen.  

If the selection frame is on the last image in the series, the message Last series is displayed in the status bar of the main window.  
 

Tip: The navigation works differently if you are working in Stack mode or Stripe mode.
Tip: As all of the other functions, the navigation functions can be executed from the main menu.
