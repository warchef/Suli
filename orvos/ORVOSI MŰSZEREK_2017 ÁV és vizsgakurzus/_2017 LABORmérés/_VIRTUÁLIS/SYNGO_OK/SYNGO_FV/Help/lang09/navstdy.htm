Next Study

Select Scroll > Next Study in the main menu.

Stack Mode: The next study is displayed. The selection frame moves to the first series in the next study.  

Doing this on the last field in the bottom right corner of the screen moves all of the images displayed in the main window to the background and displays the first series of the next study in the upper left corner of the screen.  

Stripe Mode: The currently displayed images are cleared and all of the images in the first series of the next study displayed. The selection frame is on the first image of the series in the upper left corner of the screen.  


Previous Study

Select Scroll > Previous Study in the main menu.
   
Stack Mode: The previous study is displayed. The selection frame is on the first series of the previous study.  

Doing this on the last field in the upper left corner of the screen moves all of the images displayed in the main window to the background and displays the images from the previous series in the bottom right corner of the screen.  

Stripe Mode: The currently displayed images are cleared and all of the images in the first series of the previous study displayed. The selection frame is on the first image of the series in the upper left corner of the screen.
