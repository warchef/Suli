Replacing Patient Information
Previous  Top  Next

If you are only interested in new patient information, you can override the patient information displayed in the PatientBrowser with the new patient information. Doing this prevents the PatientBrowser from geting cluttered.  
 
·Select the file you want to view.  
 
·Click on Replace.  
 
·The previously displayed patient information is cleared and the new patient information displayed in the PatientBrowser.
