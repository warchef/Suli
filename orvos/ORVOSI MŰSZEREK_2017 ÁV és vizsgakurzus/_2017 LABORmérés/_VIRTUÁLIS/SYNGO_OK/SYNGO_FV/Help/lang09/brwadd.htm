Adding Patient Information
Previous  Top  Next

You can display data contained in different directories in the PatientBrowser, for example, to select images from examinations and checkups.  
 
·Select the file you want to view.  
 
·Click on Add.  
 
·The new patient information is displayed in the PatientBrowser. You can select the previously displayed patient information by opening the drop-down list in the Patient and Study fields.
