Selecting a Range of Images
Previous  Top  Next

You can also select a range of images (for example, series 6 to 14).

·Click on the first image in the range of images you want to select. It now has a blue frame.  
 
 
 
·Press the SHIFT key and hold it down.  
 
·Click on the last image in the range of images you want to select.  
 
 
 
·All of the images between the first and the last image in the range are selected.
