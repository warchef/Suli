·Select the images you want to view. 
 
 
   
·Click on Open  
 
or  
 
·Double-click on the selected images  
 
or  
 
·Click on the selected images, and, holding down the mouse button, drag them to the main window and release the mouse button (drag & drop).  
 
The new patient information is displayed in the PatientBrowser.  
 
You can select the previously displayed patient information by opening the drop-down lists in the Patient and Study fields.  
        
The images are loaded and displayed in the main window. See Loading Images from Open File.  
 
The loading sequence is independent of the sequence in which you selected the images. This behavior is different from that of Loading Images from the PatientBrowser.
