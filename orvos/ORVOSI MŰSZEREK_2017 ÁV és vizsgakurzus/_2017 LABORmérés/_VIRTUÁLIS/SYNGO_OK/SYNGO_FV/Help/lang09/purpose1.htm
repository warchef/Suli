Purpose of the Application
Previous  Top  Next

The application's main purpose is to quickly and easily display medical images in DICOM format. You, the patient, can view the images that were taken of you during an X-Ray examination, and, if necessary, make them available to other specialists. The image itself cannot be changed.  
 
syngo fastView, is however, not intended for diagnostic purposes. For legal reasons, we must point out that it is not permitted to use syngo fastView for diagnostic purposes.  
 
syngo fastView is a stand-alone tool for viewing DICOM images.  
 
Main Features:  
 
·View medical images created by different modalities such as computed tomography, ultrasound, or magnet resonance therapy.  
·General display functions such as Stripe mode, Stack mode, and other formats.  
·Image optimization functions such as zooming (magnify/minify) and panning, the ability to change window values (image contrast and brightness) and perform distance measurements.  
·Interactive mode to view a series of images manually (interactive) or automatically as a movie.  
·Conversion functions such as Save As Bitmap and Save As AVI.  
 
syngo fastView is not a medical device and may hence not be used for diagnostic purposes. Copies of examination images and syngo fastView may be given to patients on CD or DVD for their personal use only.  

A normal viewing session could look as follows:

·Select the optimal screen size to display images.  
·Browse through images, series, and studies.  
·Select and process images (for example, windowing).  
·Dynamically display images or series of images (Interactive mode).  
·Convert processed images to other formats (for example, JPEG).
