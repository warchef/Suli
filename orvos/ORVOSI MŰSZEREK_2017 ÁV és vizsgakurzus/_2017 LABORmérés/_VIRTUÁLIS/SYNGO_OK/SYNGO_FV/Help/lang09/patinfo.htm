The Patient Information Field provides an overview of the loaded studies, series, and images:

 


In this example, this means:

·Studies: The images of four different studies are loaded. In the main window, the second loaded study is selected. The selection frame is on a series of the second of four loaded studies.  
 
·Series: Of the selected study, eight series are loaded. The series that was loaded second is selected. The selection frame is on the second of eight loaded series.  

·Images: All of the loaded images are displayed.
