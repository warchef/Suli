Selecting Multiple Series
Previous  Top  Next

To select multiple series, for example, for windowing:

·Click on an image. It now has a blue frame. 
 
 
 
·Press the CTRL key and hold it down.  
 
·Click on another image that does not have a blue frame to select it.  
 
 
 
· The other images remain selected. Clicking on a selected image deselects it.
