·CD/DVD with pre-installed syngo fastView  
Inserting the CD or DVD into your CD or DVD drive and starting syngo fastView automatically starts the search for images and displays the results in the PatientBrowser.  

·CD/DVD with DICOM images 
Inserting a CD into your CD drive automatically starts the search for images and displays the results in the PatientBrowser.  
   
 
To compare images on several CDs or DVDs, transfer one of the folders to your PC, select it using Browse for Folder and add it to the PatientBrowser.   

Removing the CD/DVD from the drive clears all of the patient's image information in the PatientBrowser.
