Selecting Images
Previous  Top Next

syngo fastView is a user-friendly tool. To achieve this, the selection of images has been designed in such as way that other modes such as windowing, minifying, and panning can be used without having to deactivate the selection mode.

·An image is selected by clicking on it.  
 
·If, however, you move the mouse while holding down the left mouse button, you do not select the image, but activate functions such as Zoom or Pan.  
 
·You can see that an image has been selected by its blue selection frame. You can also select several images at a time. See Selecting Multiple Series and Selecting a Range of Images.  

Tip: An action such as changing window values, (gray tones), is applied to all of the selected images.
