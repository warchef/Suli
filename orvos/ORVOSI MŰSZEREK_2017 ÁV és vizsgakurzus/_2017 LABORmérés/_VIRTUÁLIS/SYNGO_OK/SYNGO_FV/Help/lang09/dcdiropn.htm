·Select the file you want to view. 
   
·Opening a DICOMDIR displays the images in the PatientBrowser.  
      
 

There are a number of ways to open a DICOMDIR:

·Click on Open  
 
or  
·Double-click on  
 
or  
·Click on , and, holding down the mouse button, drag it to the main window and release the mouse button (drag & drop).  
 
The PatientBrowser is automatically opened and displays the new patient information.  
 
You can select the previously displayed patient information by opening the drop-down list in the Patient and Study fields. 
 
You can now select the desired patients/studies/series and load the images.
